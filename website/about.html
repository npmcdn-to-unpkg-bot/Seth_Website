Seth Reed

    Chain Maille is an ancient art of armor making and a present day art of jewelry making. I render Bicycle Chains into several usable pieces and combine titanium, stainless steel or copper rings to make partially recycled art. I've recreated a handful of chain Maille weaves into bicycle Maille weaves and currently know over 30 weaves using only rings.
