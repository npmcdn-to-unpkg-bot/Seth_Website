Erica Wolfson

Much of my painting begins with inspiration from the landscapes. Many have been started en plein air, on location here in Sonoma County. I feel blessed to live here and witness its beauty.

Through my artist filter my inner eye speaks. This process allows for abstraction and often an inner landscape unfolds. The irony is that I paint to ground myself and escape my life all at once. Painting is a vehicle to an intimacy with myself that is meditative and something connected to a bigger quiet mystery. Some of these paintings are purely from my imagination, an evolution of a deeper image. I like to think of them as soulscapes.

Please enjoy.
